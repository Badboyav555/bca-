# Interlacing

# Introduction

Interlacing:
```text
Old video display technique
```
hai.

Isme:
- screen lines
- alternate order mein display hoti hain.

---

# Working of Interlacing

Pehle:
- odd lines display hoti hain

phir:
- even lines display hoti hain

---

# Advantages

1. Less bandwidth
2. Old TVs ke liye useful

---

# Disadvantages

1. Flickering
2. Lower quality
3. Motion blur

---

# Non-Interlacing

# Introduction

Non-interlacing:
```text
Progressive scanning
```
bhi kehlata hai.

Isme:
- saari lines ek sath display hoti hain.

---

# Advantages

1. Better quality
2. Smooth motion
3. No flickering

---

# Real Life Example

Modern LCD aur LED TVs:
```text
Non-interlaced display
```
use karte hain.

---

# Difference Between Interlaced and Non-Interlaced

| Interlaced | Non-Interlaced |
|---|---|
| Alternate lines | Full frame |
| Old TVs | Modern displays |
| Less quality | Better quality |
| Flickering possible | Smooth display |
